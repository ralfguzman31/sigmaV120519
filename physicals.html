Physicals

Sigma Health & Wellness is able to perform required physicals for school, sports, camp, and work. Just stop in when it’s convenient for you, and we’ll get you in and out, and ready to participate.

When your child needs a school, sports, or camp physical, head into our Gilbert clinic. Our friendly medical team is here 8am-8pm 7 days a week and can make sure your child is healthy and able to safely join in activities.

School, Sports, and Camp Physicals

During the exam, a Sigma Health & Wellness provider will review your child’s medical history, perform a comprehensive physical exam, and provide useful information on injury prevention. We can also complete and sign participation or school forms. Just bring the necessary forms along for your visit, and if your child wears glasses, bring those too.
