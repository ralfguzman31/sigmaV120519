Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to my appointment?
Bring your insurance card and a photo ID.
What ages do you treat?
We treat all patients ages 6 months and up.
How much does an urgent care visit cost?
A flat rate urgent care visit costs $157. ER visits can run more than twice this amount! See a full list of our accessible pricing here.
What will I need to pay upon checking in?
Insured patients will be expected to pay their outlined urgent care co-payment upon checking in. Self-pay patients will be charged $157 upfront for a clinical visit, and additional charges may apply for other services.
Do you accept self-pay patients?
Yes. Sigma Health and Wellness offers affordable self-pay rates in an effort to make high quality healthcare accessible to everyone, regardless of their insurance plan or lack thereof.
What conditions do you treat?
Sigma Health and Wellness treats a variety of minor injuries and illnesses, including cuts, rashes, broken bones, urinary infections, cold and flu, ear infections, and more. Learn more about our services here.
What are the qualifications of your medical staff?
We have a team of licensed doctors, physician assistants, nurses, medical assistants, and technicians available to help care for you when you need it most.
