Allan Buenaventura
MSN, FNP-BC
Started Sigma Health and Wellness Clinic as a place for people of all ages to receive both medical and wellness treatment in a friendly and comfortable environment. His goal is to provide best medical care and promote wellness to community members to not only maintain and attain good health but to improve their overall quality of life. Allan Buenaventura is board-certified family nurse practitioner and received his MSN degree at University of Phoenix. He completed his undergraduate at Grand Canyon University and graduated magna cum laude. He has over 17 years of acute health care experience with background in emergency and critical care before becoming a nurse practitioner. He also held several leadership roles during his nursing career. He is a member of Sigma Theta Tau International Honor Society of Nursing, American Nurses Association, American Association of Nurse Practitioners, Arizona Nurses Association, Arizona Chapter of Gerontologic Advanced Practice Nurses Association, and is a proud US Army Veteran. He enjoys movies, outdoors, and travel with his wife and 3 children.
